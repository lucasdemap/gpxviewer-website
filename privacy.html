Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

GPX Viewer (“the app”) is developed by the GPX Viewer team. This policy explains what information the app and website collect and how it is used.

App data

Routes and GPX files you import stay on your device unless you choose to export or share them. Location data is used only when you enable location features to show your position on the map.

Analytics

The app and website use Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics to understand usage and improve the product. Analytics data is aggregated and does not include the contents of your GPX files.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple and Google. We do not receive your payment card details.
