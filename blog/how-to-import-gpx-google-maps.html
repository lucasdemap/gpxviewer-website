Import GPX into Google Maps

Use Google My Maps on desktop — or GPX Viewer on your phone.

July 23, 2026

Google Maps on mobile does not import GPX directly. On desktop, use Google My Maps: create a map → import → upload your .gpx file.

Better on mobile

For on-trail navigation, skip the desktop workaround — open the file in GPX Viewer on iPhone or Android. See open GPX on any device.
