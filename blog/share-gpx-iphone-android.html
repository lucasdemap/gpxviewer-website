Share GPX Between iPhone and Android

Send .gpx files so everyone in your group has the same route.

July 31, 2026

GPX works on both iPhone and Android — no conversion needed. Send the .gpx file via Mail, WhatsApp, AirDrop, or Google Drive.

How to share

  • iPhone — open in GPX Viewer → Share → Mail or AirDrop
  • Android — Share → Gmail, Drive, or WhatsApp
  • Recipients open with GPX Viewer — see Android and iPhone guides
