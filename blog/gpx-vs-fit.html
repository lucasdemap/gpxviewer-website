GPX vs FIT

Open route files vs Garmin's binary activity format.

July 20, 2026

GPX is text-based and widely shareable — perfect for routes. FIT is Garmin's binary format for recorded activities with rich sensor data, but harder to open outside Garmin ecosystem.

Rule of thumb

  • Share a planned route → export GPX
  • Archive a recorded ride with power/HR → FIT
  • Preview before a group ride → GPX in GPX Viewer
