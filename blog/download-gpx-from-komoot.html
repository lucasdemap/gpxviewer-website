Export Komoot tours and navigate with GPX Viewer.

July 26, 2026

In Komoot, open your tour → Export → choose GPX. Save the file and open it in GPX Viewer for map preview, distance, and elevation.

Why both apps

Komoot is great for planning; GPX Viewer is built for quick mobile viewing and navigation. Offline maps available with Pro — see offline GPX.
